2026 - Interactive kinetic sound installation

The Wave

An audience-activated installation in which a motorized push rod repeatedly disturbs water. The generated wave hits a sensor and triggers audio, making the viewer's movement part of the wave's physical and sonic formation.

Medium
Acrylic water tank, motor, electric control board, metal, 3D printed parts, speaker
Dimensions / Duration
Variable

2025 - Sound object

Recorded Rings

Tree rings make time visible. This work pairs natural growth marks with vinyl-like grooves, translating voice into code and then into acrylic form to consider how memory changes as it moves across media.

Medium
Acrylic, fixed turntable, wood, speaker
Dimensions
Variable

2025 - Mechanical sound installation

Storm City

A constructed acoustic city that recreates wind, rain, and storm sounds through mechanical devices: wind chimes, rotating discs, a rain stick, fans, and friction-based sound mechanisms.

Medium
Wood, motors, aluminum, metal balls, sailcloth, fans, 3D printed parts, speaker
Dimensions / Duration
Variable

2024 - Video work - 4:52 minutes

Subconsciousness

A video work about anxiety, depression, and the difficulty of describing mental health from the inside. The film guides viewers to observe the inner world of a young person from a third-person perspective.
